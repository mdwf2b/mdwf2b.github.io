Margie Mohun

Hello! My name is Margie, which, as you probably know, is short for Margaret. Just to keep things interesting however, my parents decided to go with a slightly different pronounciation, and I'm so glad they did! Instead of saying Margie with a soft 'g' like you would normally hear, they called me Margie with a hard 'g', just like it's pronounced in the name Margaret. So really it makes more sense to pronounce it that way. I've always really liked my name, and I think our names are a huge part of our identity, so I'm grateful my parents decided to think outside the box a little.
